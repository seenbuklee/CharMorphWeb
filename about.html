Our Story

Creating the future of open-source 3D character creation

About Us

Blender Character Project started in Early 2022, to provide a community to projects like Charmorph and its various characters. Our goal is to provide high quality open source parameterized character models that not only competes with closed source solutions but surpass them to be equivalent with quality seen in blockbuster Movies and next-gen video games.

We have become a testbed for various projects and characters, With official projects like Charmorph and Vitruvian being developed with these goals in mind, we wish to work with skilled artists and programmers to further push open-source characters to the quality users have come to expect.
